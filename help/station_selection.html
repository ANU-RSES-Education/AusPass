How to download AusPass data for a selection of seismic stations

The AusPass data portal allows you to download data for a selection of stations and events. The simplest way to download data is through station selection.
  • Step 1: To start the station selection, click on the "Explore Stations" tab.
  • Step 2: In the "Stations controls" panel on the left of the screen, select the array of your choice using the "Network Code" dropdown menu.
  • Step 3: Click on "Search" at the bottom of the page.
  • Step 4: Select the stations of your choice in the "Event and Station list" at the bottom of the screen. The station(s) selected appear(s) in a darker color on the map. Information about each station can be obtained by clicking on the map. Stations can also be selected/unselected when clicking on them on the map.
  • Step 5: Click on the "Submit request" tab at the top of the page.
  • Step 6: Enter the period during which you want to select the data. The period selected should be part of the array lifetime, given on the array webpage. Links to array webpages are provided on the AusPass homepage, and follow the url format: "http://auspass.edu.au/networks/arrayname.html"
  • Step 7: Select the type of seismic data (waveform or metadata) you want.
  • Step 8: Indicate your email address.
  • Step 9: Click on "Submit" at the bottom of the page.
  • Step 10: Click on the "Download data" tab at the top of the page.
  • Step 11: Click on "Start".
  • Step 12: Click on "Save".
Congratulations, your download is complete!
