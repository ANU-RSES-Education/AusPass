AusPass help page


AusPass is a service dedicated to the acquisition, management and distribution of passive seismic data aquired in Australia.
AusPass data can be accessed either through a standard FDSN request or though the WebDC3 graphical explorer.
We provide here several tutorials showing how to :

  • Use the FDSN webservices (international standard for seismological data) to download AusPass data: Click here
  • Search, download, process and visualize AusPass data with ObsPy (client tool implementing FDSN webservices in Python): Click here
  • Explore and download AusPass data for a selection of seismic stations with the WebDC3 graphical explorer : Click here
  • Explore and download AusPass data for a selection of seismic events (earthquakes) with the WebDC3 graphical explorer : Click here
  • Explore and download AusPass metadata for a selection of seismic arrays : Click here

The AusPass website is under construction. Please, let us know if you are having trouble accessing our data. We will do our best to help you.
Your feedback is highly appreciated and will help us to improve our service.
